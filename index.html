    💧 Hydration & ORS Calculator

    Calculate daily baseline fluid needs and estimated ORS requirements for dehydration management.

    📋 How to Mix ORS: Dissolve 1 entire packet of WHO-standard ORS in 1 Liter (5 cups) of clean drinking water. Mix well. Do not add sugar, honey, juice, or milk. Use the solution within 24 hours of preparation.
    💧

    Enter child's weight and dehydration parameters to view hydration needs.

    ⚖️ OTC Medication Dosage Guide

    Calculate exact weight-based syrup and drop dosages for common pediatric over-the-counter medications.

    ⚠️ Double-Check Strength: Always look at your medicine bottle's box to confirm the strength matches your selection. Giving incorrect concentrations is a major source of dosing errors.
    ⚖️

    Enter child's weight and select medication to calculate safety parameters.
